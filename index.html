Password Reset

If you cannot login to any of the service and want to reset your password.

  • Keep your password complex and minimum 8 characters long.
  • Use Special characters like – @ # $ % ^ ! + ( ) &
  • Use mix of upper case and lower case.

After you change your password wait for 30 mins for the password to be sync on all systems.
